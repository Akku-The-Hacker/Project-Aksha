# Privacy Policy – Aksha

Aksha respects user privacy.

The application stores routine and activity data locally on the user's device. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared with external servers.

Aksha does not track users, display advertisements, or collect analytics data.

Optional features such as Google Drive backup are only used when the user explicitly chooses to back up their data.

All routine information remains under the user's control.
